Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ):


  • Why can't I find my location?

    Our location data is limited to the United States and specifically for weather station information from wunderground.com. In the future we plan on expanding our service to cover more countries.


  • Why can't I find information for a particular piece of produce?

    Our produce database is ever expanding. If you can't find information for a particular produce you can either contact us directly and we will add your request to our database, or you can submit a request to add to our database directly.


  • What is a grow zone?

    A grow zone is a standard created by the USDA by which gardeners and growers can determine which plants are most likely to thrive at a location. The map is based on the average annual minimum winter temperature, divided into 10-degree F zones.

    More information can be found here.


  • What is an airport zone?

    An aiport zone is the airport code for the closest airport. Most airports provide some sort of weather tracking, and these zones are used by NOAA for weather service reporting.
